Our Approach

Design Process

01

Discovery

Understanding client requirements, surveying the space, and presenting a comprehensive Due Diligence Report.

02

Concept

Developing design concepts supported by 3D visualization and recommending the optimal planning approach.

03

Documentation

Preparing technical data sheets, Bill of Quantities, project scheduling, and detailed technical drawings.

04

Execution

Deploying a single point of contact ensuring quality, quantity, and project timelines with vetted vendors.

05

Handover

Pre-handover client review, complete documentation, and certified Testing & Commissioning Reports.
